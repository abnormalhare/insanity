Topic: What is Chinese Painting?

Chinese painting is probably the oldest artistic traditions in the world.

It is painted in the style of guóhuà, which means national or native painting.

The two main techniques this type of painting are Gongbi and Ink and Wash

Landscape was considered the highest form of painting in Chinese culture, and to this day is still.

In the early days of China, they normally painted ornamental things, like patterns

Now, they have very beautiful art and are very well known for it too.
